Privacy Policy

Glaude — Last updated: May 21, 2026

Glaude ("we," "our," or "us") is a mobile application that lets you browse and edit GitHub repositories using natural language, powered by Claude AI. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • GitHub OAuth Token — When you sign in with GitHub, we receive an access token that lets the app read and write to your repositories on your behalf. This token is stored exclusively on your device using the operating system's encrypted secure keychain. We never transmit your token to our own servers.
  • Repository and File Content — To fulfill your edit requests, the content of the file you select is sent to Anthropic's Claude API along with your natural language instruction. This data is transmitted over HTTPS and is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • No Account Registration — We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information beyond what GitHub provides during OAuth.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you with GitHub and access the repositories you choose to open.
  • To send your selected file content and instruction to Claude AI and return the edited result.
  • To commit the AI-generated changes to GitHub when you explicitly tap "Commit."

We do not use your information for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond operating the app's core features.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • On-device only. Your GitHub access token is stored in the device keychain (iOS Secure Enclave-backed storage). It is never stored on our servers.
  • No backend servers. Glaude has no proprietary backend. All API calls go directly from your device to GitHub's API and Anthropic's API over encrypted HTTPS connections.
  • No logs. We do not log, retain, or analyze your queries, file contents, or repository activity.

4. Third-Party Services

Glaude integrates with two third-party services. Their data practices are governed by their own privacy policies:

  • GitHub — GitHub Privacy Statement
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — Anthropic Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Because we store no data on our own servers, there is nothing for us to delete on your behalf. To remove all locally stored data, sign out of the app or uninstall it — this clears your GitHub token from the device keychain.

6. Children's Privacy

Glaude is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
